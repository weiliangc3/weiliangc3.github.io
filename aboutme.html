Interests and other things

After high school in Singapore, I moved permanently back to London and studied medicine at King College London and worked at a charity for a while. While I love working with and helping people, I’ve always wanted to design and make things, so I decided to gain the skills to make things I love and joined a web development boot-camp. I enjoyed it immensely, building things and being able to express different creative ideas, and am continuing my journey learning and improving the way I make things.

I've always had a very wide set of interests- I used to play field hockey, swim and play ultimate frisbee, debate in inter-secondary-school debates, present my projects at national science fairs, be active in the creative writing scene in Singapore (writing poetry), I have a black belt in karate, and I even sung (badly) in a comedy band. I've always been interested in technology, and always been geeky- reading comics, Isaac Asimov sci-fi/fantasy novels and playing around on computers.

I'm often super curious, and always willing to try new things. I try to be in a position where I'm a little out of my comfort zone, because I think that's the only way to grow, and I love solving puzzles and problems where I struggle to find the right answers.
