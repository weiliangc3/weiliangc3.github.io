Fight Federation

Fight Federation

Fight Federation is a tournament management platform that allows people to find tournaments and join them, while also recording the results of the games, and recording the winners. The app is designed to cater to office tournaments where the game schedules may be fluid depending on other commitments, and handles the administrative aspects of running tournaments such as listing matches and calculating points.
