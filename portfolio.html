Fight Federation

Fight Federation

Fight Federation is a tournament management platform that allows people to find tournaments and join them, while also recording the results of the games, and recording the winners. The app is designed to cater to office tournaments where the game schedules may be fluid depending on other commitments, and handles the administrative aspects of running tournaments such as listing matches and calculating points.

I used: Mongoose | Express.js | AngularJS | Node.js | Bourbon | SCSS | Grunt | AngularUI Router | Multer

Take a look at it here: https://fightfederation.herokuapp.com/

Or at the Github page here: https://github.com/weiliangc3/wdi-project-4

Wiki Wars

Wiki Wars

Wiki wars is our take on the Wikipedia game where the challenge is to navigate from one Wikipedia article to another in the least clicks or the least amount of time, using entirely links within Wikipedia. The project was done as part of Electron's 23:00:01 Hackathon, a 23hour and one second hackathon. This project was super fun to do, especially with the team of Peter Le and Ryan Thomas Didier McWard.

We used: Mongoose | Express.js | AngularJS | Node.js | MediaWiki’s API

Take a look at it here: https://pwrwikiwars.herokuapp.com/

Or at the Github page here: https://github.com/weiliangc3/wikiwars

Street Smart

WeiDings

WeiDings

A web-app for creating and managing wedding gift registries. The project focus was on streamlining the user experience and providing a mobile-friendly user interface. The user registration system relies on verification e-mails and no e-mail service is attached to the project from Heroku yet.
