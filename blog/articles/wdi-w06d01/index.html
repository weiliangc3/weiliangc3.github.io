First Ruby on Rails day

Written by Wei

Internet died for the morning so we got stuck doing nothing for quite a while. Looked at some websites and did a small quiz. Then we installed rails and basically trivialised all the homework that had come before.

Then we learned to scaffold, and do all sorts of rails magic. All the printing of the partials etc etc.

I don’t really know what to say, today was a blitz of learning Rails, and really we learned a lot of what happens when we do stuff, but it’s a lot of stuff that we were writing to begin with. Like learning a lot of shortcuts, and a lot of new rules we have to follow to use Rails.

Then I did homework, didn’t plan, had no focus, had no purpose, and flubbed it. Very inefficient, and really poor.
